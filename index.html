Game of Pig

Two players. The player whose turn to roll the dice. The total is added to the current player's cumulative total unless either die comes up as a "one ".

If this happens, this player's turn is over , the cumulative total zeroed out and the other player's turn .

After each roll , the current player can either roll again,(assuming a "one" was not rolled) or if the current player feels that the luck is running thin, then can pass to the other player and the cumulative total will be added to the score.

The first player to get higher score than Check Point wins.

Oh, and if you roll two "ones" (snake eyes) , your current score gets zeroed out . So ,beware!.
